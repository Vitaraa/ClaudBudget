Privacy Policy

How ClaudBudget handles your personal and financial information — what we collect, why, and the control you keep over it.

Last updated June 1, 2026
On this page

    ClaudBudget is a personal budgeting tool. Your numbers are yours — we collect only what the product needs to work, and we never sell your data.

    01 Overview

    This policy explains what information ClaudBudget collects when you use the app, how that information is used, and the choices you have. It applies to your account, the accounts and transactions you connect, and the projections ClaudBudget builds from them.

    By creating a ClaudBudget account you agree to the handling of information described here. If you do not agree, please don't use the service.

    02 Information we collect

    We keep collection narrow and purposeful. In broad terms:

    Account details
    Your name and email, and a securely hashed password if you don't sign in with Google.
    Financial data
    Balances, transactions, budgets, and the life-event plans you add to build your Foresight projection.
    Connection data
    Read-only tokens from any bank or institution you link, held only to refresh your balances.
    Usage & device
    Basic, aggregated logs — pages viewed, errors, browser type — used to keep the app reliable.

    We do not collect your full bank credentials, and we never store payment-card numbers ourselves.

    03 How we use your information

    • To run the product — show your balances, categorize transactions, track budgets, and compute your net-worth projection.
    • To keep your account secure — detect unusual sign-ins and protect against abuse.
    • To improve ClaudBudget — understand which features help, using aggregated, de-identified usage.
    • To reach you — send essential account, security, and service messages.
    No advertising. ClaudBudget does not run ads, and we do not use your financial data to target you or build advertising profiles.

    04 Sharing & disclosure

    We don't sell your personal information. We share it only in these limited cases:

    • Service providers who host our infrastructure or power bank connections, under contracts that bind them to protect your data.
    • Legal requirements, where we're compelled by valid legal process or must protect the rights and safety of our users.
    • With your direction, when you explicitly ask us to export or share specific data.

    05 Data retention

    We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal and financial data within 30 days, except where we're required to retain limited records to meet legal obligations.

    06 Your rights & choices

    Wherever you live, ClaudBudget aims to give you meaningful control:

    • Access & export — download a copy of your data at any time.
    • Correction — edit or fix the information in your account.
    • Deletion — close your account and have your data removed.
    • Disconnect — unlink any connected institution, which revokes our read access immediately.

    07 Cookies

    ClaudBudget uses only the cookies it needs to keep you signed in and remember your preferences, such as your theme. We don't use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

    08 Changes to this policy

    We may update this policy as the product evolves. When changes are material, we'll let you know in the app or by email before they take effect. The date at the top always reflects the latest version.

    Your financial life deserves serious protection. Here's how ClaudBudget keeps your data safe — described plainly, without the jargon.

    01 Our approach

    Security isn't a feature we bolt on — it shapes how ClaudBudget is built. We collect as little as we can, protect what we hold, and give you read-only connections so the app can see your balances without ever being able to move your money.

    02 Encryption

    In transit
    Every connection to ClaudBudget is encrypted with TLS, so your data is protected as it travels between your device and our servers.
    At rest
    Sensitive data and connection tokens are encrypted in our database using industry-standard ciphers.
    Passwords
    Stored only as salted, one-way hashes — never in plain text, and never visible to our team.

    03 Protecting your account

    • Sign in with Google to lean on an account you already keep secure.
    • Sensible defaults — minimum password length and checks against unusual sign-in activity.
    • Session control — sign out anywhere, and sessions expire after inactivity.

    04 Infrastructure

    ClaudBudget runs on reputable cloud infrastructure with regular backups, restricted internal access on a need-to-know basis, and monitoring to catch problems early. Access to production systems is limited and logged.

    05 Bank connections

    Read-only by design. When you link an institution, ClaudBudget receives a limited, read-only token to refresh your balances. We can't initiate transfers or payments, and you can revoke access at any time by disconnecting the account.

    06 Responsible disclosure

    If you believe you've found a security vulnerability, we want to hear from you. Email us with the details and we'll investigate promptly. We appreciate researchers who report issues responsibly and give us a chance to fix them before going public.

    07 Your role

    Security is a partnership. You can help by using a strong, unique password, keeping your email account secure, and disconnecting any institution you no longer use. If something looks off in your account, tell us right away.

    Report a security concern

    We respond to disclosures promptly.
